The Agenda

DocType HTML5 will have 5 sessions. Each session will be for 45 minutes followed by 10 minutes for Q and A.

  • HTML5 101
  • Covering the basics
  • Demo time - Geolocation, Offline Storage, Canvas
  • Multimedia Kit
  • Bling Bling - A showcase of HTML5 AWESOME

HTML5 101

The basics of HTML5. Doesn't matter if you are grokking HMTL5 code for breakfast or just wetting your feet this session is for you. This session will tell you what is it, why is it important, who stands to gain and more. We will also cover who built this, who is responsible, what is possible and what are we going to cover in the following sessions.

  • What is HTML5?
  • How do we reach here? Answering the question where we were with HTML/XHTML and now HTML5 and not XHTML2
  • W3C Standards versus the browser people
  • So what is possible with this shiny new thing
  • A brief list what to follow in the next four sessions

Covering the basics

Starting with the simple doctype. What is the HTML5 tech landscape made up of. A brief overview canvas, geolocation, video, offline web applications, CSS3, drag and drop and elements/tags for semantic code

Demo time - Geolocation, Offline Storage, Canvas

Applications that run on mobiles, newer browsers can find your geographic location. Other applications can provide a richer experience provided they can store their data in the browser. We will demo all of this and more. You will also get a chance to download the code being demoed to your laptops.

  • Where you at? Geolocation magic with pizza ordering application and Trafficam
  • Offline Storage

Multimedia Kit

Its like the late nineties. When there was a shift in PC hardware vendors from selling separate multimedia kits ( external plug-ins ) to everything integrated and working out of the box.

What is possible with HTML5 multimedia but what are the pitfalls. What all you should be careful about being too bleeding edge. This will answer in a small manner if Flash is going away or what are the alternatives.

  • HTML5 Audio
  • HTML5 Video

Bling Bling - HTML5 Showcase

You have an idea what HTML5 is and what you might be able to build with it. This session will show you what are the coolest projects people are building currently. The latest, greatest working HTML5 code. Games with realistic physics, shiny media galleries and more. If you are still sitting on the fence about the whole HTML5 hype this session will dazzle you and get you truly excited about what you can start building.

  • Quake in the browser and other HTML5 games
  • Business applications
